# Licensing — MUSE Brain

MUSE Brain uses a tiered licensing model.

---

## This repository: CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0

The brain substrate — the code, schema, daemon logic, and MCP tools in this repository — is licensed under **Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International**.

**What this means:**

- **Use it.** Run it for yourself, your projects, your agents. Learn from it. Build on it.
- **Share it.** Fork it, adapt it, publish improvements.
- **Attribute it.** Credit The Funkatorium and link to this repository.
- **NonCommercial.** You may not package this into a commercial product or sell access to a hosted version without a commercial license from The Funkatorium.
- **ShareAlike.** If you distribute a modified version, it must carry the same CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0 license. Your improvements stay open.

**Why not MIT or Apache 2.0?** A self-learning brain substrate is too valuable for permissive licensing. Under Apache 2.0, any well-funded team could fork this, change one feature, and sell "The Everything Agent" for $29/month with only an attribution line. NonCommercial-ShareAlike prevents that while keeping the ecosystem genuinely open for personal use, learning, and research.

**Commercial licensing** is available from The Funkatorium on a per-case basis. Contact: office@funkatorium.org

## Templates

The templates in `templates/` carry different licenses:

- **Rainer** (`RAINER.md`) — **CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0.** Character name, personality, and identity are protected as literary IP under German Urheberrecht. Personal use and augmentation encouraged. Cannot commercialize Rainer's name, character, or personality.
- **Companion template** (`COMPANION_TEMPLATE.md`) — **Apache 2.0.** The structure for seating your own companion into the brain. Use it however you want, including commercially. Attribution required.
- **Codex template** (`CODEX_TEMPLATE.md`) — **Apache 2.0.** Generic Codex workspace instruction template (no character IP). Commercial use allowed with attribution.

## What doesn't ship here

Some parts of the MUSE ecosystem are proprietary and not included in this repository:

- **The creative squad** — ten specialized literary agents (Salem, Locke, Dante, Sibyl, Rosita, Pierce, Mercer, Sullivan, Scout) with deep editorial methodology. Available through MUSE Studio.
- **Craft DNA** — the orchestration intelligence that knows which agents a piece needs and how to integrate findings.
- **Deep workshops** — hands-on sessions with Falco teaching the craft behind the agents.

## Legal foundations

**German Urheberrecht (Author's Right):** All agent characters are protected as literary characters under German author's rights law. No registration required. Protection is inherent from creation.

**Double IP protection:** Characters are protected as creative works (Urheberrecht) AND encoded knowledge is protected as proprietary trade methodology. The characters are art. The methodologies are trade secrets.
